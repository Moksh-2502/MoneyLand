About Us

Our startup is focused on developing an application that facilitates the pooling of money from different individuals to collectively invest in real estate properties. The aim is to create a platform where users can come together to invest in properties, share ownership, and potentially earn returns on their investments. We are giving an opportunity to small investors who can't invest in big properties, which often yield greater profits.
